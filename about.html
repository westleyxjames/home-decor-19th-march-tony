Our Story

Founded in 2011, PrimeNest Decor began with a simple vision: to make premium home decor accessible to everyone. What started as a small interior styling service has grown into a full-service home transformation company serving clients across the United States.

Our team of experienced designers and decorators are passionate about creating spaces that reflect your unique personality while maintaining functionality and comfort. We believe that your home should be your sanctuary - a place where you feel truly yourself.

Today, we're proud to have transformed over 1,000 homes and helped countless families create their dream living spaces. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, affordable home decor solutions that transform living spaces into personalized sanctuaries, enhancing the quality of life for our clients through thoughtful design and superior craftsmanship.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in home decor across the United States, recognized for our innovation, quality, and unwavering commitment to making beautiful living spaces accessible to everyone.
